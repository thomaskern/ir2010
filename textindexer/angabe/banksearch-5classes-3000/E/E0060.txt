People who have won the IOCCC
 H E L P !
We would like to have this page contain
current EMail addresses and home page links of people who
have written a winning IOCCC entry.
If you are a winner and have a home page, please
EMail your home page URL so
we can fill in this page.
Even if you don't have a home page, please
EMail us your EMail address.
If you are not a winner but know of a home page or current EMail
address of someone below, please
EMail it to the judges.
NOTE:
You must include the words ``ioccc question'' in the
subject of your EMail message when sending EMail
to the judges.
We did not do a major search for EMail addresses. Obviously
some of the more recent EMail addresses found in the hint
files are still valid. We picked the 'assume they are still
valid' cutoff date of Jan 1996 just to be sure.
Corrections, updates, comments and suggestions welcome.
If you are looking for the names of this year's winners,
who might find them in the who won page
if they are available.
